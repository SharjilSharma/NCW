Outraging Modesty of Women - Section 74 BNS

Legal protection against assault or criminal force with intent to outrage modesty under Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita

Section 74: Assault or Criminal Force to Woman with Intent to Outrage Her Modesty

"Any person who assaults or uses criminal force to any woman, intending to outrage or knowing it to be likely that he will thereby outrage her modesty.
