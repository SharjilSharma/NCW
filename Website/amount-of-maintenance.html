Amount of Maintenance

How maintenance amount is determined and calculated by courts

Court's Discretion

Maintenance that is considered fair, appropriate, and reasonable by the courts in normal circumstances may be granted. However, it varies from case to case, depending on the circumstances in which the petitioner and respondent live and the status of the respondent.

Factors Considered by Courts

•

Financial Status: Income and financial capacity of both parties are carefully evaluated.

•

Living Standards: The standard of living maintained during the relationship is considered.

•

Individual Circumstances: Specific needs and circumstances of each case are taken into account.

Important Note

The amount of maintenance is determined by the court based on various factors including the financial status of both parties, their living standards, and the specific circumstances of each case. There is no fixed amount as it varies case by case.
