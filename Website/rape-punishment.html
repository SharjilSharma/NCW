1.2) Punishment for Rape (Section 64, BNS)

Punishment under Section 64, BNS

Section 64 provides punishment for committing the heinous crime of rape. This section is divided into two sub-sections.

1. Section 64(1):
Provides a minimum sentence of ten years of imprisonment that may extend to life imprisonment and fine.
2. Section 64(2):
Provides punishment not less than ten years of imprisonment but may extend to imprisonment for life which shall mean imprisonment for the remainder of that person's natural life and fine.

⚖️ Important Note

The law provides severe punishment for rape with a minimum sentence of 10 years imprisonment, reflecting the gravity of this crime. Life imprisonment can mean imprisonment for the remainder of the person's natural life in certain cases.
