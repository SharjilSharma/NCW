Punishment for Cruelty

Legal penalties for cruelty to women under Section 85

Section 85 BNS - Punishment

As per the provision under Section 85 of BNS, the husband or his relative who commits cruelty against a married woman can be punished with imprisonment up to 3 years along with fine.

Punishment Details

•

Imprisonment: Up to 3 years imprisonment

•

Fine: Fine as determined by the court

•

Both: Court may impose both imprisonment and fine

Who Can Be Punished

•

Husband: The woman's husband who commits cruelty

•

Husband's Relatives: Any relative of the husband who subjects the woman to cruelty

Legal Deterrent

The punishment under Section 85 BNS serves as a strong legal deterrent against cruelty to women in marital relationships, ensuring that both husbands and their relatives can be held accountable for their actions.
