Who can claim Maintenance under various laws

Eligible Persons for Maintenance

The following categories of persons are eligible to claim maintenance under various laws:

  • •

    Wife who is unable to maintain herself - A wife who cannot support herself financially has the right to claim maintenance from her husband.

  • •

    Legitimate or illegitimate child, whether married or not, unable to maintain itself - Any child, regardless of legitimacy or marital status, who cannot support themselves is entitled to maintenance.

  • •

    Legitimate or illegitimate child (not being a married daughter) who has attained majority, where such child is, by reason of any physical or mental abnormality or injury unable to maintain itself - Adult children with disabilities or injuries that prevent self-maintenance are eligible for support.

  • •

    Father or mother, unable to maintain himself or herself - Elderly or incapacitated parents who cannot support themselves can claim maintenance from their children.

⚠️ Important Note

These provisions ensure that vulnerable family members receive necessary financial support when they cannot maintain themselves. The law recognizes the duty of family members to support each other in times of need.
