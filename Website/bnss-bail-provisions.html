Bail Provisions for Women under Section 480 BNSS

Special bail provisions and exceptions for women in non-bailable offences under the Bharatiya Nyaya Suraksha Sanhita

Section 480 of Bharatiya Nyaya Suraksha Sanhita

Section 480 of Bhartiya Nyaya Suraksha Sanhita deals with bail in case of non-bailable offences.

üö´ General Restrictions on Bail

According to Section 480, a person shall not be released on bail if:

  • Death or Life Imprisonment: There is reasonable ground to believe that he has committed an offence punishable with death or life imprisonment, or
  • Previous Conviction (Serious): He has been previously convicted for an offence punishable with death, life imprisonment, or for a term of seven years or more, or
  • Repeat Offender: He has been convicted on two or more occasions on other offences with a term between three and seven years.

Exception for Women

This Section contains certain exceptions such as the court may grant bail even in these cases, if such person is under the age of 16 or is a woman or is sick or infirm.
