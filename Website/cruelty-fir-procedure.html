Procedure for FIR or Criminal Complaint

Step-by-step process for filing complaints and FIR

Filing a Complaint for Cruelty (Section 85)

To initiate criminal proceedings against a husband or his relatives for the offence of cruelty, a First Information Report (FIR) or a formal complaint under the relevant section is mandatory.

Authorized Persons to File Complaint

The following persons are authorized to file such a complaint:

•

The aggrieved person: The married woman herself

•

Immediate family: Her father, mother, brother, or sister

•

Father's relatives: Her father's brother or sister

•

Mother's relatives: Her mother's brother or sister

•

Other relatives: Any other person related to the woman by blood, marriage, or adoption, provided they have obtained prior permission from the court

Mandatory Requirement

Filing an FIR or formal complaint is mandatory to commence criminal proceedings. The complaint must be filed by authorized persons as specified under the law.
