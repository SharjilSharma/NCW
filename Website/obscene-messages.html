Sending Obscene or Offensive Messages

Understanding harassment through digital communication

What are Obscene or Offensive Messages?

Cybercrime against women includes sending offensive messages via WhatsApp, mail, or any other social media site. When an offence does not qualify as a crime under the Information Technology Act, women usually turn to the Indian Penal Code's sections 354A for sexual harassment, 354 for outraging women's modesty, 499 for defamation, and 509 for insulting women's modesty.

Types of Offensive Messages

  • Sexually explicit messages or content
  • Threatening or intimidating messages
  • Abusive language targeting gender
  • Unwanted sexual advances through messages
  • Sharing explicit images or videos without consent
  • Persistent messaging despite being asked to stop
  • Messages intended to humiliate or degrade

Legal Framework

Indian Penal Code Sections

  • Section 354A: Sexual Harassment - Covers unwanted sexual advances and sexually explicit messages
  • Section 354: Outraging Women's Modesty - Addresses acts intended to outrage the modesty of women
  • Section 499: Defamation - Covers messages that damage reputation or character
  • Section 509: Insulting Women's Modesty - Addresses words, gestures, or acts intended to insult the modesty of women

Information Technology Act

  • Section 67: Publishing obscene material in electronic form
  • Section 67A: Publishing sexually explicit material
  • Section 66E: Violation of privacy

How to Report Offensive Messages

  1. Do not delete the offensive messages - preserve as evidence
  2. Take screenshots of all offensive content
  3. Block the sender immediately
  4. Report to the platform (WhatsApp, Facebook, etc.)
  5. File a complaint at the nearest police station
  6. Report online through National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
  7. Contact women helplines for support

Prevention and Safety Tips

  • Use privacy settings to limit who can message you
  • Be cautious about sharing personal contact information
  • Block unknown or suspicious contacts immediately
  • Report offensive behavior to platform administrators
  • Don't engage with or respond to offensive messages
  • Keep evidence of all harassment
  • Seek support from friends, family, or authorities
