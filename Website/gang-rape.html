1.4) Gang Rape (Section 70, BNS)

Gang Rape under Section 70, BNS

Section 70 lays down the punishment for gang rape. Where a woman is raped by more than one person acting in furtherance of a common intention, each of them shall be liable for the offence of rape and shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment for not less than twenty years which may extend to lifetime imprisonment which shall mean imprisonment for the remainder of that person's natural life and fine.
