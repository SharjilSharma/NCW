Constitutional Provisions

Key provisions ensuring women's welfare and legal support

Adequate Means of Livelihood

Constitutional provisions ensuring women have access to adequate means of livelihood and economic security. Article 39(a) directs the State to secure adequate means of livelihood for all citizens, including women, promoting economic empowerment and financial independence.

Equal Justice and Free Legal Aid

Access to justice and free legal aid services for women who cannot afford legal representation. Article 39A ensures equal justice and free legal aid to promote access to courts for all, especially marginalized women.

  • Legal Service Authorities provide free legal assistance
  • District Legal Services Authorities in every district
  • Eligibility based on income and social status
  • Simple application procedure for accessing services

Maternity Relief

Constitutional provisions and policies ensuring maternity benefits and support for working women. Article 42 directs the State to make provisions for securing just and humane conditions of work and maternity relief, protecting women's health and economic security during pregnancy and childbirth.
