3) Sexual Harassment (Section 75, BNS)

Sexual Harassment under Section 75, BNS

This section criminalizes unwelcome sexual behavior perpetrated by a man, which can include acts such as:

-

Making unwanted sexual advances.

-

Passing sexually colored remarks or comments.

-

Forcibly showing pornography.

-

Any other unwelcome physical, verbal, or non-verbal conduct of a sexual nature.

⚠️ Important Note

Sexual harassment is a serious criminal offense under Section 75 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita. The key element is that the conduct must be "unwelcome" - meaning it is not desired or invited by the victim.
