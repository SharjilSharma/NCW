1.5.2) Free Medical Treatment in any Hospital

Free Medical Treatment under Section 397, BNS

According to section 397 of Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, no private or government hospitals can charge fees for the treatment of rape victims. All hospitals, either private or public, shall immediately provide first aid to the victims free of cost.
