When can Maintenance Provisions be invoked

Circumstances and conditions for invoking maintenance provisions

Legal Breach and Court Petition

Whenever a law governing maintenance is breached, a petition can be filed with a court of competent jurisdiction, and the petition will be decided on the merits of the case.

Common Scenarios

•

Husband's Refusal: If a husband refuses to maintain his wife, the wife may approach the court for maintenance.

•

Domestic Violence Cases: Prima facie evidence of domestic violence is required to obtain maintenance for the wife.

•

Case-by-Case Basis: All cases are handled on their own merits within the parameters of the law.

Important Note

Each case is evaluated individually based on its specific circumstances and merits. The court considers all relevant factors before making a decision on maintenance.
