1.5.4) DNA Sampling for Forensic Testing

DNA Sampling Guidelines

The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare has issued guidelines mandating that a standardized medico-legal kit be available in every hospital for the purpose of collecting DNA samples for forensic testing.

The medical report generated from this examination must contain the following details:

-

Name and address of the survivor

-

Age of the survivor

-

A description of the biological material collected for DNA testing

-

Observation and documentation of any physical injuries, if present

-

An assessment of the general mental condition of the survivor
